# Maison Imperial Legal & Platform System

Germany-based, international-ready, luxury-level legal and platform extensions. All existing functions retained; integrations are additive.

---

## 1. Governing Law Structure

All contracts generated by the luxury document engine now include in the **footer**:

- **Governing Law:** Federal Republic of Germany  
- **Jurisdiction:** Cologne, Germany  
- **International Shipping Clause**  
- **Customs & Duties:** Responsibility of the recipient unless otherwise agreed  
- **VAT & Tax Disclosure:** Client responsible for local tax obligations  
- **Arbitration:** Optional for international clients (ICC or DIS, Cologne)  
- **Export Compliance:** Client warrants compliance with export/import laws  
- **DSGVO/GDPR:** Data processing per Platform Terms  

**Languages:** Primary English, secondary German; optional FR/AR via content generation.

## 2. Automatic Number Systems

| System        | Format              | Example        |
|---------------|---------------------|----------------|
| Product      | `AB-YYYY-CAT-XXXX`  | AB-2025-NEC-0001 |
| Contract      | `CTR-TYPE-YYYY-XXXX`| CTR-DEP-2025-0001, CTR-RSL-2025-0003, CTR-VIP-2025-0004, CTR-FRA (fractional) |
| Certificate   | `CERT-YYYY-XXXX`     | CERT-2025-0001   |
| **Registry ID** | `REG-YYYY-XXXX`   | REG-2025-0001   |

- **Registry ID** is assigned on first certificate/ownership and stored on `masterpieces.registry_id`. It appears on certificates and in the registry API.
- All identifiers are system-generated and not user-editable.

---

## 3. Payment & Ownership Logic

- **Ownership = false** until `payment_status` is effectively “paid in full” (workflow completed).
- **Deposit:** Reserves the piece and starts production; no ownership transfer.
- **Ownership transfer** only after: 100% payment, payment confirmation, certificate created, registry updated.
- Final invoice is generated automatically before delivery (existing workflow).

---

## 4. Resale Ecosystem (Controlled)

- Resale start → status `resale_review`.
- Resale contract auto-generated; commission 8% (default), VIP 6%, Royal 5%.
- Registry update prepared on platform resale completion.
- Externally sold: registry status “external transfer”, warranty void.
- Admin: Approve, Reject, Private Auction, Curated Marketplace, Maison Buyback (existing endpoints).

---

## 5. VIP Membership (€15,000)

- Annual agreement with 48h Early Access, Private Auction Room, Concierge, repair priority, reduced resale fee, invite-only events, Royal tier upgrade possibility.
- Contract generated on approval; optional auto-renewal as per platform terms.

---

## 6. Fractional Ownership

- Percentage participation; minimum investment and exit strategy defined by Maison.
- **Digital Fractional Ownership Agreement** is **automatically generated** for each participant when a fractional pool is initialized (`POST /api/admin/fractional/initialize`).
- Asset remains in Vault custody; no physical division; secondary trading possible on platform.

---

## 7. Digital Asset Registry

- **GET /api/registry/masterpiece/:id** returns:
  - `registry_id`, serial, production year, materials, gemstones  
  - Ownership history, service history, provenance  
  - **Prestige score**, **demand score**, **rarity level**, **ownership history badge** (e.g. First Owner, Multi-Owner)  
  - Value development, market status, transfer type, warranty  
- Certificate auto-generated on first purchase, resale, transfer. Registry ID and QR code (linking to registry) on certificates.

---

## 8. Service & Aftercare

- Booking types: cleaning, repair, stone upgrade, adjustment, re-polishing (stored in `service_history`).
- Service log is included in the registry response.

## 9. Maison Buyback

- Admin can send buyback offers; dynamic pricing; asset returns to Vault; re-placement later (existing buyback endpoints).

## 13. International Compliance (GDPR)

- **POST /api/gdpr/consent** — Log consent (userId, consentType, ipAddress, version).
- **GET /api/gdpr/consent/:userId** — List consents for user.
- **POST /api/gdpr/data-request** — Create data access request (export etc.).
- **GET /api/admin/gdpr/data-requests** — List all data requests (admin).
- **POST /api/admin/gdpr/data-request/:id/complete** — Mark request completed (status, responseDetails).

Tables: `consent_log`, `data_access_requests`.

## 14. Prestige System

- Registry exposes **prestige_score**, **demand_score** (from bids/views), **rarity_level**, **ownership_history_badge**.
- Resale affects prestige (existing prestige resale metrics). All exposed in **GET /api/registry/masterpiece/:id**.
